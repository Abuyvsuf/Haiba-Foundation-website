Our Story

From a Mentorship Dream to a Movement

Haiba Foundation began in the early 2000s when the late Prof. Saidi Hassan and fellow Nubian professionals looked around Kibra and refused to accept a painful reality: fewer than three students from the community were making it to university each year. In 2004, that mentorship spirit was officially incorporated into Haiba Foundation — a community home for ambition, dignity, and shared responsibility.

The word Haiba carries deep meaning in Arabic/Nubi: prestige and dignity. For our people — descendants of King's African Rifles soldiers who settled in Kibra and were denied title deeds for generations — that meaning is personal. Education became our language of resistance, belonging, and hope.

Our Founders

Prof. Saidi Hassan — The Architect

Late Associate Professor of Human Anatomy at the University of Nairobi. He started mentorship in the late 1990s with a clear vision: nurture Nubian professionals who could advocate for the community from positions of influence.

Dr. Ahmed Kalebi — Founding Trustee

Renowned pathologist and founder of Pathologists Lancet Kenya. A trusted mentor and supporter of Haiba's mission, and patron of Gogo Boys Football Club in Kibera.

What We Do

Six Pillars of Community Uplift

Every programme is designed around one belief: that celebrating and supporting excellence creates a ripple that transforms communities.

🏆 Annual Excellence Awards Flagship Programme

Each April, we gather to honour the brightest students in Kibra — those who scored C+ or above in KCSE and 350+ in KCPE. The Prof. Saidi Hassan Excellence Prize crowns our top performers.

📚 Holiday Tuition Programme

During April and August school breaks, we run free tuition classes for primary and secondary learners with volunteer teachers, alumni mentors, and academic resources.

🌱 Annual Mentorship Retreat

An intensive retreat, often in Mombasa, pairing youth with professionals. Students gain career clarity, life skills, and networks where yesterday's mentees mentor today's learners.

🌙 Ramadhan Feeding Programme

During Ramadan, we distribute food packages and host community iftars for vulnerable families in Kibra — because no child should learn hungry and no family should break fast alone.

🎓 NUSO — Nubian University Students Org.

Founded in 2014 by Haiba alumni, NUSO now leads much of our tuition and peer mentorship. It is a living, self-sustaining cycle of excellence.

🏛️ Cultural Preservation & Community Events

Through Nubian Souk bazaars, folk dance, and forums, we celebrate identity while advancing academic success. Heritage is not separate from progress — it strengthens it.
